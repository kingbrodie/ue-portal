Options to Pay Your School Fees

Option 1

UE Payment Option

PNB Bills Payment (Recommended)

Pay through PNB Mobile App / Internet Banking:

  1. Log in to your PNB mobile application or online service.
  2. Select the "Bills Payment" option.
  3. Search for "UNIVERSITY OF THE EAST" as the Biller.
  4. Input your Student Number as the Subscriber Number.
  5. Enter the Amount and confirm the payment.
  6. Save a copy of your proof of payment (screenshot/transaction slip) and email it to the University Finance Office.
Option 2

Bills Payment through

Mobile Banking Applications (BDO, Metrobank, BPI, etc.)

Via FUND TRANSFER (Metrobank, BPI, BDO, etc.):

  1. Log in to your chosen bank's mobile application or online service.
  2. Select "Fund Transfer" or "Send Money."
  3. Transfer to the University's designated bank account (Details at Finance Office).
  4. Crucial: In the description/remarks field, input the Student's Full Name and Student Number.
  5. Email a clear copy of the successful transaction to the Finance email for confirmation.
Option 3

E-Wallet Payment

GCash Bills Payment

GCash Bills Payment Steps:

  1. Log in to your GCash mobile application.
  2. Select the "Bills" icon, then choose "Schools."
  3. Search for "UNIVERSITY OF THE EAST" as the Biller.
  4. Enter the required details (Student Name, Student Number, Amount Due).
  5. Confirm the payment. Save the text/email confirmation and email it to the Finance Office.
